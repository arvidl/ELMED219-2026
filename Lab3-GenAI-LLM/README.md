# Lab 3: Generative AI and Large Language Models

This lab introduces students to generative AI and large language models in biomedical applications.

## Learning Objectives
- Understand the fundamentals of generative AI
- Learn about large language models and their applications in medicine
- Practice using ChatGPT and other AI assistants for biomedical tasks
- Explore ethical considerations in AI-assisted research

## Materials
- Introduction to Generative AI notebook
- ChatGPT for Biomedical Research notebook
- NotebookLM for Literature Review notebook
- Ethical AI in Medicine notebook

Complete all notebooks and submit a reflection on the use of AI assistants in biomedical research.
